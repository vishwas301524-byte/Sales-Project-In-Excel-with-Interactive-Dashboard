# 📊 Store Data Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

An interactive **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze **Vrinda Store's 2022 sales performance**. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, helping stakeholders understand customer behavior, sales trends, and business performance.

# 📌 Project Objective

The objective of this project was to build an **interactive Excel dashboard** to analyze **Vrinda Store's annual sales performance (2022)** and generate actionable insights that help improve sales strategies for **2023–2024**.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- 📈 Track overall sales performance
- 👥 Understand customer purchasing behavior
- 🌍 Identify top-performing states
- 🛒 Analyze sales channels
- 📦 Monitor order status
- 🏷 Evaluate product categories
- 📊 Make data-driven business decisions

---

# 📂 Dataset

**📌 Source:** Vrinda Store Sales Data (2022)

### 📋 Dataset Includes

- 🧾 Order Details
- 👤 Customer Demographics (Age & Gender)
- 🛍 Product Categories
- 🌐 Sales Channels
- 📦 Order Status
- 📍 State-wise Sales Data
- 📅 Monthly Sales Records

---

# ❓ Business Questions Solved

The dashboard answers the following business questions:

- 📊 Compare **Sales vs Orders** in a single visualization.
- 📅 Identify the **highest-performing month** by sales and order volume.
- 👩 Analyze **gender-wise purchasing behavior**.
- 📦 Understand the **distribution of order status**.
- 🏆 Find the **Top 10 states** contributing to total sales.
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Explore the relationship between **Age Group and Gender**.
- 🛒 Identify the **top-performing sales channels**.
- 🏷 Determine the **highest-selling product category**.
- ✅ Calculate the **percentage of successfully delivered orders**.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

> 👩 **Women contribute approximately 65% of total purchases.**

> 🏆 **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh generate the highest sales revenue.**

> 👨‍👩‍👧 **Customers aged 30–49 years contribute nearly 50% of total orders.**

> 🛒 **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the top-performing sales channels.**

> 📦 **Over 90% of customer orders are successfully delivered.**
